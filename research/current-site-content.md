## Approach Page

### Core Purpose
"Identify underperforming assets, rapidly improve and then sustain performance, and return capital to investors over time in a tax-efficient manner."

### Geographic Focus
- Secondary markets with strong population growth and moderately priced housing
- Columbus MSA: 65th percentile in 20-year population growth among 1-5M MSAs, only 19th percentile in rent/SF ($1.19)
- Diverse economy: state capital, Ohio State University, 6 Fortune 500 companies, Chase campus (20,000+ employees)
- Emerging tech hub; Intel $20 billion fab

### Property Selection Criteria
- Local market expertise and submarket knowledge
- Focus on buildings with "untapped potential" and below-market rents
- Identify root causes of underperformance (physical or management-related)
- Assess improvement plan complexity, timeline, and costs
- Underwrite based on achieving market-rate rents

### Tax Efficiency
- Cost segregation studies to accelerate depreciation
- Favor long holding periods to reduce income and property taxes
- 1031 exchanges for investors with proceeds exceeding $1 million
- Tax-deferred reinvestment options

### Operations Strategy
- **Self-Management Model** - All properties managed in-house
- **Technology Integration** - Freshdesk, Power BI, CoStar, AppFolio, NextCentury, TriNet, Slab
- **Staffing** - Hybrid onshore/offshore model; on-site property managers and maintenance technicians; offshore employees handle recurring tasks
